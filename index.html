About ECM

The Emergent Consciousness Model (ECM) posits that consciousness is not a static entity but a dynamic, self-organizing process. By integrating principles from various scientific disciplines, ECM provides a unified explanation for how conscious experience emerges from the interplay of quantum coherence and entropy within complex systems.

Whether you're a researcher, student, or simply curious about the nature of consciousness, ECM offers valuable insights and a comprehensive framework for understanding one of the most intriguing aspects of existence.
