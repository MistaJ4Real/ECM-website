Theoretical Foundations

The Theoretical Foundations section establishes the core scientific and philosophical principles underpinning ECM. It introduces two main pillars: Quantum Coherence in Complex Systems and Entropy and Dynamic Balance.

Quantum Coherence in Complex Systems

Quantum Coherence in Complex Systems explores how quantum phenomena like wave-particle duality, superposition, and entanglement facilitate unified behavior in complex systems such as the brain, potentially giving rise to consciousness.

Entropy and Dynamic Balance

Entropy and Dynamic Balance delves into the role of entropy as a measure of disorder and its balance with order within open systems. It discusses how the interplay between entropy and order is crucial for the emergence and maintenance of consciousness, aligning with the Second Law of Thermodynamics and principles of dynamic equilibrium.

Together, these foundations provide a multidisciplinary basis for understanding how consciousness can emerge from the interplay of quantum mechanics and thermodynamic principles within complex systems.
